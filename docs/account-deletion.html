Account Deletion

You can delete your MewScore account at any time. This page explains how to do it and what data will be deleted.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the MewScore app
  2. Go to the Profile tab
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete my account"
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your account information (email, user ID)
  • Your profile data (gender, age preferences)
  • Your scan history and analysis results
  • Your subscription status

What Data Is NOT Stored

Photos sent for facial analysis are never stored on our servers. They are processed immediately and deleted right after analysis.

Data Retention

Account deletion is immediate and permanent. We do not retain any of your personal data after deletion.

Warning: Account deletion is irreversible. You will lose all your scan history and any active subscription benefits.
